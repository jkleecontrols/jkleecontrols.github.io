Howdy!

My name is Jaekyung Lee, and I can easily go by Jackie. I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. at Texas A&M University in the Autonomous Systems Lab. My research primarily focuses on the exciting fields of unmanned vehicle routing and scheduling, path planning, autonomous control, and dynamic control, with a particular emphasis on UAVs (Unmanned Aerial Vehicles).

I am passionate about developing innovative solutions that push the boundaries of what autonomous systems can achieve. My work involves creating advanced algorithms and models that enable unmanned vehicles to navigate complex environments efficiently and safely. I strive to contribute to the field of autonomous systems by enhancing the capabilities of UAVs and other unmanned vehicles, making them more adaptable and reliable for various applications.

Here, you can explore my research interests, projects, and publications. Whether you are a fellow researcher, a potential collaborator, or just someone curious about autonomous systems, I hope you find the information here insightful and inspiring.

Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or would like to discuss potential collaborations. I am always open to new ideas and opportunities to advance the field of autonomous systems. Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to connecting with you!

Best regards,
