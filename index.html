Howdy! I'm Jackie Lee.

I’m currently pursuing my Ph.D. at Texas A&M University in the Autonomous Systems Lab, focusing on autonomous vehicle routing, scheduling, and dynamic control. I’m seeking an internship for Spring/Summer 2025 to apply my skills and further my research in these exciting fields.

If you’re interested in potential collaborations or have any questions, feel free to reach out. I’m always open to discussing new ideas and contributing to advancing autonomous systems.
