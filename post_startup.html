DIY Drone Production Kit Sharing Platform for Drone Enthusiasts

Leadership

September, 2021 Jackie Lee

I led a team of four in developing a concept for a DIY drone production kit sharing platform aimed at empowering drone enthusiasts. This platform allows users to share, customize, and build drones using modular kits, promoting a collaborative community of makers and innovators in the drone hobbyist space.

Our platform aims to democratize drone production by providing access to modular kits and detailed instructions, enabling users to assemble drones tailored to their specific needs and preferences. By fostering a community where knowledge and resources are shared, we hope to inspire creativity and lower the barriers to entry in drone building and customization.

Key aspects of the project included leadership in project planning, coordinating team efforts, and aligning our objectives with the needs of the target audience. Throughout the development process, we focused on creating a user-friendly interface and ensuring that the kits are accessible and easy to assemble, even for beginners.
